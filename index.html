About Yunisha and Yugesh Kirana Pasal

Established in 2015, Yunisha and Yugesh Kirana Pasal has been serving the Pokhara community with quality products and exceptional service. We pride ourselves on offering a wide range of groceries, household essentials, and personal care items at competitive prices.

Our friendly staff is always ready to assist you in finding exactly what you need, making your shopping experience pleasant and convenient.
